Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the "Ma" Calendar app (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, created as a Freemium service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

This privacy policy is effective as of 2026-03-05.


Information Collection and Use

The Application collects the following information when you use it:

  • Account information: Your email address and password, used to create and manage your account via Supabase Authentication.
  • Google Calendar data: If you choose to connect your Google Calendar, the Application accesses your calendar events (read and write) to display your schedule and create events on your behalf. Google OAuth tokens are stored securely in our database and are only used to interact with your calendar.
  • User-created content: Tasks, goals, projects, weekly plans, and scheduling preferences you create within the Application.
  • AI interactions: Messages and context you provide when using AI planning features. This content is sent to Anthropic (Claude) to generate responses and is also stored as chat history in our database to provide continuity across sessions.
  • User memory and preferences: Scheduling preferences, work hours, and contextual notes you provide to personalise AI planning (e.g. "I have family commitments on Fridays").
  • Device and usage data: IP address, operating system, time and date of use, and pages visited within the Application.
  • Payment information: If you subscribe, payment is processed by Stripe. The Application does not store your credit card details. Stripe provides us with subscription status only.

Third Party Services

The Application uses the following third-party services that may receive your data:

  • Supabase – Database, authentication, and backend infrastructure. Stores your account, tasks, goals, calendar tokens, and preferences. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude) – AI language model used to generate planning suggestions. Your tasks, goals, and calendar context are sent to Anthropic to generate responses. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Calendar API – Used to read and write calendar events when you connect your Google account. Google Privacy Policy
  • Stripe – Payment processing for subscriptions. Stripe Privacy Policy

The Service Provider may also disclose your information as required by law, to protect rights or safety, or to respond to legal requests.


Google Calendar Access

The Application requests access to your Google Calendar to read existing events and create new events on your behalf. This access is used solely to provide scheduling features within the Application. You may disconnect your Google Calendar at any time from the Settings screen, which will delete your stored Google OAuth tokens. The Application's use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.


Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or request deletion, your data will be removed from our systems within a reasonable time. To request deletion, contact us at tergel@uni.minerva.edu.


Opt-Out Rights

You can disconnect Google Calendar at any time in Settings. You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application. To delete your account and all associated data, contact tergel@uni.minerva.edu.


Children

The Application is not directed at children under the age of 13. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact tergel@uni.minerva.edu.


Security

The Service Provider takes reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage of credentials and tokens, and server-side handling of all API secrets. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and the Service Provider cannot guarantee absolute security.
